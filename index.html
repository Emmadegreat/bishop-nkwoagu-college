WHO WE ARE

Welcome to Mabel Divine International School (MDIS), a beacon of educational excellence in Nnewi, Nigeria. Established in 2008, MDIS stands as a testament to a decade of commitment to nurturing young minds. Our school, located at 1 Mabel Divine Close, Off Chief Izundu Rd, Abubor, Nnewichi, embodies a holistic approach to education.

With a dedicated faculty, state-of-the-art facilities, and a rich tapestry of academic and extracurricular programs, MDIS is more than an institution; it's a community fostering growth, knowledge, and character. Join us on a journey where learning knows no bounds, and aspirations find wings. Welcome to MDIS – where excellence is a tradition.

At MDIS, we pride ourselves on providing a vibrant and inclusive learning environment where students not only acquire knowledge but also develop essential life skills. Our commitment to fostering creativity, critical thinking, and global awareness sets MDIS apart. With a focus on personalized learning, cutting-edge technology, and a values-driven curriculum, we empower students to explore their passions and reach their full potential.

Our Facilities

At Mabel Divine International School, we provide a cutting-edge education with state-of-the-art facilities, including well-equipped science labs, a rich library, and a modern computer lab. Elevate your learning experience with us!

Computer Lab

Our secondary school computer lab is a state-of-the-art facility equipped with cutting-edge technology. It provides students with hands-on experience in coding, programming, and digital literacy, fostering a dynamic learning environment for the exploration of modern technology.

Science Lab

Our science laboratory is a hub for scientific exploration. It is well-equipped with advanced apparatus, enabling students to engage in hands-on experiments across physics, chemistry, and biology. The lab promotes inquiry-based learning, encouraging students to develop critical thinking skills..

Library

The library is a quiet haven for intellectual growth. Stocked with a diverse collection of books, it offers students a tranquil space for research and independent study. Our library serves as a resourceful center, nurturing a love for reading and fostering a culture of lifelong learning.
